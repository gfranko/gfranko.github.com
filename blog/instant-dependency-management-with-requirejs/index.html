Instant Dependency Management With RequireJS

Jul 21st, 2013

In May 2013, Packt Publishing published my Require.js book, Instant Dependency Management with RequireJS. This book is perfect for intermediate to advanced JavaScript developers who are interested in learning more about AMD and Require.js.

Topics

Some of the topics covered include Require.js configurations, creating AMD modules, using third-party libraries with Require.js, the mobile web, Lodash templates, Jasmine unit testing, and r.js (the Require.js optimizer).

Free Preview

A free preview and sample chapter are both available. Happy reading!
